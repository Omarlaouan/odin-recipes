Autumn mocktail

Ingredients:

  • tube of black icing
  • 50g pumpkin or butternut squash, peeled and deseeded
  • 1 ripe pear
  • 250ml orange juice
  • 1cm thick slice ginger

Steps:

  1. Decorate two tumblers to look like Halloween pumpkins with the tube of black icing â€“ pipe triangles for the eyes and noses and zig zags for the mouths. Leave to dry.
  2. Cut the pumpkin into smaller chunks and put in a blender along with the pear, orange juice, ginger and 100ml cold water.
  3. Blend until smooth then pour into the prepared glasses.
